Location

Nestled within the tranquil embrace of the oak tree-laden landscape, the ambiance of the Brejuinha river beach setting beckons you to immerse yourself and uncover the quintessential dancefloor nestled beneath the verdant canopy.

Ascending slightly from the festival grounds, a meticulously equipped campsite awaits, poised to elevate your experience to new heights. Modern conveniences are at your fingertips: from well-maintained toilets and invigorating showers to readily accessible water points and power outlets, we have curated a range of thoughtful upgrades that are certain to enhance your stay.

Brejuinha - Feel The Nature offers more than just a gathering; it's an opportunity to commune with nature's beauty while indulging in the rhythm of the music. Join us in this idyllic haven where the convergence of nature and celebration creates an unforgettable tapestry of experiences.
